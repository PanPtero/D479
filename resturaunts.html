Grocery

Sunny Side Grocers: Discover a cozy spot offering fresh ingredients, local specialties, and everyday items to keep your island adventure running smoothly.

TaniTreasures Grocery: Find unique local products, beverages, and essentials at this charming shop, perfect for souvenirs and essentials alike.

Supermarkets

Stock up on everything you need with a wide selection of local produce, snacks, and essentials. Tropical Fresh Market and Island’s Best Supermarket are global chains and are sure to have what you're craving, when you're craving it.

Night Owl Convenience

Always open, day or night, for snacks, drinks, toiletries, and last-minute needs—your reliable companion at any hour. Always open and ready to serve, Night Owl Convenience is your go-to shop for everything you need at any hour. Whether you’re craving a midnight snack, need fresh beverages, toiletries, or emergency essentials, this friendly store has you covered. Located conveniently in the heart of Taniti, it’s perfect for travelers who keep late hours or need quick supplies after a day of exploring the island. Reliable, accessible, and stocked with a variety of local and international treats, Night Owl Convenience ensures you’re never far from what you need, no matter the time.

Local Favorites

  • Seaside Serenity: Savor the freshest catch of the day paired with fragrant rice, all set against the tranquil ocean backdrop. A true taste of Taniti’s coastal charm.
  • The Tidal Table: Dive into authentic local flavors with expertly prepared fish dishes and fluffy rice, perfect for a laid-back island feast.
  • Ocean’s Bounty: Experience the bounty of Taniti’s waters with deliciously grilled fish and savory rice, served in a cozy seaside setting.
  • Harbor House: Enjoy traditional fish and rice specialties that have been passed down through generations, offering a genuine taste of island life.
  • Crab & Coast: Indulge in fresh seafood and hearty rice bowls, crafted with love and the day’s best catches in a welcoming atmosphere.

American Dining

  • Liberty Eats: Satisfy your craving with juicy burgers, crispy fries, and classic American comfort foods in a lively, friendly setting.
  • The Big Apple Diner: Experience a slice of home with hearty breakfasts, milkshakes, and all-American favorites that will make you feel right at home.
  • Star-Spangled Grill: From sizzling steaks to smoky BBQ ribs, enjoy hearty American cuisine with a tropical twist, perfect for fueling your island adventures.

Dishes of the Pacific

  • Lotus Blossom: Discover the vibrant flavors of Asia with beautifully crafted sushi, stir-fries, and aromatic curries in a serene setting.
  • Dragon Pearl: Embark on a culinary journey with authentic Chinese, Thai, and Vietnamese dishes, blending bold spices and fresh ingredients.
